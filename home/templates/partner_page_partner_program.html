HUBNETIX Partner Program

Hubnetix Partner Program is designed to empower our partners, distributors, and system integrators with the resources and support they need to drive mutual growth. Our program offers various partnership levels to accommodate businesses of all sizes and expertise, ensuring a collaborative and rewarding relationship.

Hubnetix Partner Program is designed to empower our partners, distributors, and system integrators with the resources and support they need to drive mutual growth. Our program offers various partnership levels to accommodate businesses of all sizes and expertise, ensuring a collaborative and rewarding relationship.

Why Partner with Hubnetix?

  • Access to a wide range of premium products.
  • Competitive pricing and attractive margins.
  • Marketing and sales support to help you grow.
  • Training and certification programs.
  • Dedicated account management.

Partner Levels:

Hubnetix Master Partner

Partners with the highest level of support and stock availability.

  • Role:

    Main stockist

  • Responsibilities::

    • Maintain substantial inventory to support regional demand
    • Provide logistics and distribution support
    • Ensure availability of products to other partners

  • Benefits:

    • Exclusive pricing and priority stock allocation
    • Marketing support and joint promotions
    • Dedicated account manager

  • Requirements:

    • Minimum stock volume commitment
    • Sales target achievement

Hubnetix Strategic Partner

Regional partners with access to a broad portfolio of products.

  • Role:

    Regional partner

  • Responsibilities::

    • Regional sales and distribution
    • Relationship management with key customers
    • Market development within assigned region

  • Benefits:

    • Regional exclusivity
    • Joint branding opportunities
    • Access to special promotions

  • Requirements:

    • Regional presence with an established network
    • Annual revenue commitment

Hubnetix Premier Plus Partner

Special partners with access to top-tier support and priority treatment.

  • Role:

    Special partners

  • Responsibilities::

    • Strategic collaboration on key projects
    • Exclusive focus on high-value opportunities
    • Industry thought leadership

  • Benefits:

    • Exclusive deals and privileges
    • Executive-level engagement
    • Invitation to special events

  • Requirements:

    • Strategic partnership approval
    • Proven track record of success

Hubnetix Premier Partner

A higher-level partner with added support and access to more exclusive products.

  • Role:

    Higher level partner

  • Responsibilities::

    • Drive large-scale projects
    • Engage in strategic marketing initiatives
    • Provide top-tier customer support

  • Benefits:

    • Increased margin incentives
    • Premium support access
    • Co-branded marketing initiatives

  • Requirements:

    • High revenue commitment
    • Strong market presence

Hubnetix Certified Partner

Registered partners with certification after completing specific training.

  • Role:

    Registered partner with certification

  • Responsibilities::

    • Meet certification requirements through training
    • Offer technical support and implementation
    • Drive product awareness

  • Benefits:

    • Certification badge and credentials
    • Advanced technical training
    • Lead generation support

  • Requirements:

    • Successful completion of certification training
    • Service capability demonstration

Hubnetix Value Partner

Registered partners who have access to competitive pricing and key products.

  • Role:

    Registered partner

  • Responsibilities::

    • Resell Hubnetix products within their market
    • Provide basic support to customers
    • Follow Hubnetix pricing guidelines

  • Benefits:

    • Access to standard pricing and discounts
    • Sales and technical training
    • Marketing materials support

  • Requirements:

    • Registration approval
    • Sales commitment

Program Benefits Across Levels:

  • Competitive pricing models
  • Marketing and branding support
  • Sales and technical training
  • Exclusive partner portal access
  • Priority technical support

How to Join:

Interested businesses can apply to join the Hubnetix Partner Program by submitting their application through our official website or by contacting our partner management team.

The Hubnetix Partner Program is tailored to create mutual success and long-term growth. We look forward to partnering with businesses that share our vision and commitment to delivering top-tier solutions in the market.
